Cabo is a card game that requires from the player memory and manipulation. 
Each player is given 4 random cards out of which he can only view the 2 before the game starts.
When the game starts, each player takes a turn and chooses a random card from the deck.
After the player chooses a card they have 3 options:
  a) Discard the card and dump it to the pile (which is viewed by everyone)
  b) Exchange the card with a card from their own pile (they do not necessarily have to know their card's identity)  
  c) If it is a choice card, exploit its ability (we'll get to that later)
The goal of the game for each player is to minimize the sum of the 4 cards (or less) in their own pile.
